CDB Burger Bonanza

The finest of trash bin burgers placed on a lightly cigarette dusted bun with no complimentary water.

Ingredients

  • Organic surprise mince mash
  • Seven scrapings of authentically aged dust cheddar
  • Macca's Finest Ketchuppe Packets

Instructions

  1. Firmly shape the surprise mince mash, add seasoning of choice. (We recommend aged Vape Juice)
  2. Toss and wash
  3. Carefully slice and lay on toppings, soggy chips are a prime addition.
  4. Serve on a paper plate with a touch of sour milk and Macca's Finest Ketchuppe
